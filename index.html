About Envirostruct Solutions

Jharkhand's Trusted Partner for Environmental Compliance

Envirostruct Solutions Pvt Ltd is a NABL accredited testing laboratory based in Ranchi, serving industries, government bodies, and institutions across all of Jharkhand. We specialize in environmental monitoring, industrial hygiene, and compliance testing.

Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with advanced analytical instruments operated by experienced scientists, ensuring accurate, traceable results that stand up to regulatory scrutiny.

Our Capabilities

Testing Services

From air and water monitoring to structural assessments and geotechnical investigations — a representative look at our work across Jharkhand.

Ambient Air Quality

PM2.5, PM10, SO₂, NOₓ, CO, O₃, and VOCs monitoring as per CPCB standards.

  • ✓ PM2.5 & PM10 Analysis
  • ✓ Gaseous Pollutants (SOₓ, NOₓ)
  • ✓ RSPM & TSPM

Stack Emission Testing

Comprehensive flue gas analysis from industrial stacks including particulate matter and heavy metals.

  • ✓ Particulate Matter (PM)
  • ✓ SO₂, NOₓ, CO emissions
  • ✓ Heavy Metals in stack gas

Water & Wastewater

Physical, chemical, and biological analysis of drinking water, surface water, and industrial effluents.

  • ✓ BOD, COD, TDS, pH
  • ✓ Heavy metals & pesticides
  • ✓ Microbiological analysis

Construction Materials

Quality testing of cement, aggregates, bricks, steel, and tiles as per BIS/IS standards for project compliance.

  • ✓ Cement & aggregate testing
  • ✓ Brick, tile & block testing
  • ✓ Steel & rebar quality

Geotechnical Investigation

Sub-surface soil investigation for foundation design, slope stability assessment, and site suitability.

  • ✓ Borehole drilling & sampling
  • ✓ SPT & CBR testing
  • ✓ Foundation design support

Non-Destructive Testing

In-situ structural assessment of buildings, bridges, and infrastructure — without damaging the structure.

  • ✓ UPV (ultrasonic pulse velocity)
  • ✓ Rebound hammer test
  • ✓ Rebar detection & cover
