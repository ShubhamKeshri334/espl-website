Who We Are

Ranchi's Premier Environmental Testing Partner

Envirostruct Solutions Pvt Ltd is a NABL accredited environmental testing laboratory based in Ranchi, Jharkhand. We were established with a singular purpose: to deliver laboratory results that industries, government authorities, and communities can rely on.

Our facility is equipped with advanced analytical instruments — including atomic absorption spectrometers, gas chromatography systems, and continuous ambient air monitoring stations — all operated under strict ISO/IEC 17025 quality protocols.

From water quality compliance to industrial stack emission audits, we serve industries, EPC contractors, government agencies, and research institutions across Jharkhand and beyond.

Accreditation

NABL Accredited Laboratory

Envirostruct Solutions Pvt Ltd is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) under ISO/IEC 17025. Our accreditation number is .

NABL accreditation is India's highest quality benchmark for testing laboratories. It demonstrates our technical competence, measurement traceability, and adherence to internationally recognised quality management standards. Our reports are accepted by CPCB, State PCBs, courts, and government departments across India without question.
