Key Analytical Equipment

Our laboratory is equipped with precision instruments calibrated to national standards, ensuring measurement traceability for every test.

Atomic Absorption Spectrometer (AAS)
Heavy metal analysis (Pb, Cd, Cr, Cu, Zn, Ni, As, Hg) in water, soil, and biological samples.
UV-Vis Spectrophotometer
Colorimetric analysis — BOD, COD, phosphate, nitrate, colour, and turbidity in water samples.
Sound Level Meter & Noise Dosimeter
Class 1 precision for ambient, occupational, and industrial noise measurements per IS/OSHA standards.
High Volume Air Sampler (HVAS)
PM10 and TSPM collection for ambient air quality monitoring as per CPCB guidelines.
BOD Incubator
Temperature-controlled incubation for 5-day Biochemical Oxygen Demand (BOD₅) in water and wastewater.
Stack Emission Sampler (Isokinetic)
Isokinetic sampling train for particulate matter, gaseous pollutants, and heavy metals in industrial stacks.
Multi-gas Analyser
Real-time measurement of SO₂, NOₓ, CO, CO₂, and O₂ in flue gases and ambient air.
Ion Chromatograph (IC)
Simultaneous determination of anions (Cl⁻, SO₄²⁻, NO₃⁻, F⁻, PO₄³⁻) in water samples.
Muffle Furnace & Hot Air Oven
High-temperature sample preparation — ash content, ignition loss, and gravimetric testing.
