Our Capabilities

Our Testing Services

NABL accredited testing across environmental, construction, geotechnical, and industrial domains — conducted with precision and delivered with speed across Jharkhand.

Infrastructure & Construction

Civil & Infrastructure Testing

Testing of construction materials, sub-surface conditions, and structural integrity for buildings, roads, and infrastructure projects across Jharkhand.

Construction Material Testing
Quality testing of cement, aggregates, bricks, tiles, and steel as per BIS/IS standards.
Materials Tested
  • Cement — strength, setting time, consistency
  • Aggregates — gradation, specific gravity, abrasion
  • Concrete — compressive strength, water absorption
  • Bricks & Blocks — strength, water absorption
  • Steel / TMT — tensile, yield, elongation, bend test
Applications
  • ✓ Quality control at construction sites
  • ✓ Government project acceptance testing
  • ✓ Pre-tender material compliance checks
  • ✓ Third-party quality audits for contractors
Concrete Mix Design
IS 10262 based mix design for M20 to M60 grade concrete with specified workability requirements.
Design Parameters
  • Target mean compressive strength
  • Water-cement ratio determination
  • Aggregate proportioning
  • Workability — slump / flow
  • Trial mix & 7-day / 28-day strength
Applications
  • ✓ RCC structures, bridges & foundations
  • ✓ Pavement quality concrete (PQC)
  • ✓ M20 to M60 — pump & normal concrete
  • ✓ Pre-production mix approval
Bituminous Mix Design
Marshall method mix design for BC, DBM, and other bituminous layers as per MORTH/NHAI specs.
Marshall Method Parameters
  • Optimum Bitumen Content (OBC)
  • Marshall stability & flow value
  • Air voids (%)
  • Voids in Mineral Aggregate (VMA)
  • Voids Filled with Bitumen (VFB)
Applications
  • ✓ National & state highway construction
  • ✓ NHAI / MORTH specification compliance
  • ✓ Bituminous overlay design
  • ✓ Contractor pre-production QC
Geotechnical Investigation
Sub-surface investigation and soil testing for foundation design, slope stability, and site suitability.
Tests (IS 2720 Series)
  • Grain size analysis
  • Atterberg limits — LL, PL, shrinkage
  • Proctor compaction (Light & Heavy)
  • California Bearing Ratio (CBR)
  • Unconfined compressive strength
  • Direct shear / Triaxial shear
Applications
  • ✓ Building & bridge foundation design
  • ✓ Road sub-grade assessment
  • ✓ Mining slope stability studies
  • ✓ Site suitability for regulatory approvals
Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)
In-situ structural assessment without damaging the structure — for buildings, bridges, and infrastructure.
NDT Methods
  • Rebound Hammer — surface hardness & strength
  • Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) — crack detection
  • Core Cutting & Testing — in-situ strength
  • Rebar Locator / Cover Meter
  • Carbonation Depth Test
Applications
  • ✓ Building structural health assessment
  • ✓ Bridge & flyover condition surveys
  • ✓ Pre-demolition / renovation audits
  • ✓ Insurance & litigation support reports
Pavement / Structure Evaluation & Design
Condition assessment and structural design for roads, highways, and infrastructure projects.
Evaluation Methods
  • Pavement Condition Index (PCI) — ASTM D6433
  • Benkelman Beam Deflection — IRC 81
  • Rutting & Cracking Survey
  • Skid Resistance (BPN) — ASTM E303
  • Layer Thickness (GPR / coring)
Applications
  • ✓ Road rehabilitation & maintenance planning
  • ✓ NHAI / PWD quality compliance
  • ✓ Highway overlay design
  • ✓ Urban road condition surveys
Environment & Industry

Environmental Testing

NABL accredited environmental monitoring, industrial testing, and compliance services across air, water, soil, noise, and energy sectors.

Ambient Air Quality
CPCB/MoEF compliant monitoring of outdoor air pollutants for EIA, compliance, and baseline studies.
Parameters Tested
  • PM2.5 & PM10 — IS 5182 Part 23
  • SO₂, NOₓ, CO — IS 5182 methods
  • NH₃, H₂S, Ozone (O₃)
  • Meteorological parameters
Applications
  • ✓ EIA/EMP baseline studies
  • ✓ Consent to Operate compliance
  • ✓ Court order / NGT reports
Stack Emission Testing
Isokinetic flue gas sampling from chimneys, boilers, and DG sets for PCB submissions.
Parameters
  • Particulate matter — IS 11255 Part 2
  • SO₂, NOₓ, CO, HCl, HF
  • Heavy metals (Hg, Pb, Cd) — USEPA 29
  • Flue gas velocity & temperature
Applications
  • ✓ Cement, steel & power plants
  • ✓ Consent to Operate renewals
  • ✓ DG sets >800 kVA compliance
Water & Wastewater Analysis
Drinking water, borehole, effluent, and STP/ETP outlet testing per BIS/CPCB standards.
Parameter Groups
  • Physical — pH, TDS, turbidity, TSS
  • Organic — BOD, COD, TOC
  • Heavy metals — Pb, Cd, Cr, As, Hg
  • Microbiological — coliform, E. coli
Applications
  • ✓ Potability reports (IS 10500)
  • ✓ Effluent discharge compliance
  • ✓ River & groundwater monitoring
Noise & Vibration Testing
Ambient noise mapping and occupational vibration dosimetry per IS/CPCB/OSHA standards.
Measurements
  • Laeq ambient noise — CPCB / IS 3483
  • Leq occupational TWA — OSHA
  • Whole-body & hand-arm vibration
  • Peak sound pressure level
Applications
  • ✓ EIA noise impact assessment
  • ✓ Occupational health compliance
  • ✓ Construction site monitoring
Soil & Solid Waste Testing
Soil characterisation and solid waste analysis for contamination, SWM compliance, and agricultural suitability.
Parameters
  • pH, EC, OC, N, P, K — fertility profile
  • Heavy metals — Pb, Cd, Cr, As, Hg
  • TCLP leachate — USEPA Method 1311
  • TPH, PAH, organic contaminants
Applications
  • ✓ Contaminated site assessment
  • ✓ Hazardous waste classification
  • ✓ Agricultural soil fertility testing
Coal Testing Services
Proximate analysis, calorific value, and statutory testing for Jharkhand’s mining and energy sector.
Test Parameters
  • Moisture, Ash, Volatile Matter — IS 1350-1
  • Gross Calorific Value — IS 1350 Part 2
  • Total Sulphur — IS 1350 Part 3
  • Hardgrove Grindability Index — IS 9867
Applications
  • ✓ Power plant fuel quality control
  • ✓ Coal grade verification & procurement
  • ✓ Mining statutory compliance
Environmental Consultancy
End-to-end EIA, CTO/CTE applications, compliance audits, and regulatory support for industries.
Services Offered
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Environmental Management Plan (EMP)
  • Consent to Establish / Operate (CTE/CTO)
  • Annual environmental statements
Regulatory Coverage
  • ✓ JSPCB & CPCB standards

  • ✓ NGT compliance reporting
